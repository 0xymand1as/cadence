The Reference Customer

How Wish You Were Here uses Cadence.

WYWH is a Los Angeles live-music session series produced by Bigger Dreams. Every episode involves coordinated posts across the show's TikTok, the featured artist's TikTok, and our production company's TikTok during a 7-day release window. Cadence was built to solve that — for ourselves first, now for everyone running the same play.

Episode drop day

Show account leads with full episode reveal at 12 PM PT. Artist account follows with their performance clip at 4 PM. Production house drops behind-the-scenes at 8 PM. Three accounts, one campaign, zero collisions.

Trailer week

7 days before drop, Cadence schedules teaser clips across 4 accounts on a staggered 12-hour cadence. The algorithm sees consistent activity. The audience sees momentum.

Post-drop tail

3 days after drop, performance dashboard surfaces the top-performing clip per account. Cadence drafts the next-rollout brief from what worked.

How It Works

Three steps from sign-up to scheduled.

1

Connect your TikToks

Sign in once per account via TikTok's official OAuth. Add as many accounts as your rollout covers. No password sharing. No screen-mirroring tools.

2

Upload your videos

Drag videos in, write captions (or use brand templates), set the schedule. Cadence enforces gap rules across all your accounts automatically.

3

Cadence publishes

At each scheduled time, the post fires through TikTok's Content Posting API. You get a confirmation. The post goes live. No human in the loop.
