Pricing FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the free trial work?

14-day full access to your chosen tier. No credit card required. Downgrade to Free or cancel anytime — no charges.

What's a "connected TikTok account"?

Each unique TikTok handle you connect via OAuth. If you manage 3 artists, that's 3 accounts. The Pro tier covers up to 5; Studio covers up to 20.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from Settings → Billing. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period; no pro-rated refunds.

Do you support Instagram / YouTube / Facebook?

TikTok is our flagship platform today. Instagram + YouTube + Facebook support is on the roadmap for Q4 2026.
