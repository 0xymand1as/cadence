About

A working production company built the tool.

Cadence is operated by Bigger Dreams, LLC — a Los Angeles production company filming music videos, brand campaigns, and live sessions for indie labels and major brands (HBO, Patron, Adidas, Soundcloud) since 2020.

The flagship customer is Wish You Were Here, our live-music session show. Every episode rolls out across three TikToks — the show, the featured artist, and our production handle — over a seven-day window. Coordinating that manually was painful. So we built the tool.

Now we are opening it to other music production companies, indie labels, and artist managers running the same multi-brand rollouts.
