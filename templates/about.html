Our Story

Built by the team behind Wish You Were Here.

Cadence isn't a side project from a SaaS company. It's a tool we built for ourselves — to run a live-music show that requires posting across 5+ TikTok accounts every week — and decided to open up to everyone running the same play.

The Parent

Bigger Dreams.

Bigger Dreams is a Los Angeles production company founded by Jake Douglas in January 2020. Six years of brand-client work — HBO, Patron, Adidas, SoundCloud — plus music video credits with Peso Pluma, YG, and Rosalía. We produce, we direct, we ship.

Bigger Dreams is the parent company behind Cadence. Same team, same standards, same care for craft.

The Show

Wish You Were Here.

WYWH is our flagship live-music session series. Real artists, multi-cam coverage, room-mic mixed by Slowsie Music, distributed across IG, YouTube, TikTok, and Facebook. Every episode triggers a coordinated 7-day rollout across the show's accounts, the featured artist's accounts, the production company's accounts, and the producer's accounts.

That rollout problem is what Cadence was built to solve.

Why We Built This

Generic schedulers weren't enough.

Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, Metricool — we tried them all. None of them understood that a music rollout is fundamentally different from a marketing campaign. None of them surfaced same-hour collisions across multiple accounts under the same campaign. None of them spoke to the workflow of an artist + label + production team coordinating in real time.

So we built Cadence. For ourselves first. Now for everyone else running the same play.
